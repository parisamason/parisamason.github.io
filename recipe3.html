Corn Ribs

Corn ribs are a crunchy, flavorful way to enjoy corn on the cob. They curl like ribs when cooked, and they make the perfect snack, appetizer, or side dish.

Below are the ingredients, directions, and fun twists you can try.

Jump to Ingredients

History of the Dish

Corn ribs became popular on TikTok, where home cooks discovered that slicing corn into rib-like strips creates a fun, crispy snack. The idea is inspired by Mexican street corn and Southern grilled corn.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Cilantro (optional)
  • Parsley flakes (optional)

Recipe & Directions

  • Cut each ear of corn into quarters lengthwise.
  • Place the corn strips in a large bowl.
  • Mix olive oil and all seasonings together.
  • Coat the corn evenly with the seasoning mixture.
  • Air fry at 400°F for 12–14 minutes or bake at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.
  • Top with lime juice, cilantro, parsley, and parmesan.

Twists You Can Make

Elote Corn Ribs

Toss with chili powder, cumin, smoked paprika, and lime. Top with cotija cheese and cilantro.

Creole & Cajun Corn Ribs

Use Cajun seasoning or a mix of cayenne, garlic powder, and onion powder.

Thai Curry Corn Ribs

Season with Thai spices like ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
