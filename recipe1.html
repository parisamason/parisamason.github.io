Corn Ribs

History of the Dish

Corn ribs are a modern twist on traditional grilled corn. The snack became popular on platforms like TikTok, where home cooks discovered that slicing corn into rib-like strips creates a fun, crispy treat. The technique is inspired by Mexican street corn and Southern-style grilled corn, combining bold spices and flavorful toppings.

Learn more at: Corn Ribs

Recipe

Corn ribs are a crunchy, flavorful way to enjoy corn on the cob. The corn is cut into curved strips, seasoned, and baked or air-fried until the edges curl like ribs. Perfect as a snack, appetizer, or side dish.

Ingredients

  • 4 ears of corn
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt & pepper
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Cilantro (optional)
  • Parsley flakes (optional)

Directions

  1. Cut each ear of corn into quarters lengthwise.
  2. Place the corn strips in a bowl.
  3. Mix olive oil and seasonings.
  4. Coat the corn evenly.
  5. Air fry at 400°F for 12–14 minutes or bake at 425°F for 20–25 minutes.
  6. Serve with lime, cilantro, parsley, and parmesan.

Twists You Can Make

  • Mexican Elote Style: Chili powder, cumin, lime, cotija cheese, cilantro.
  • Cajun or Creole: Cajun seasoning, cayenne, garlic, onion powder.
  • Thai Inspired: Ginger, garlic, red pepper flakes, Thai basil.
